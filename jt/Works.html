The Saltsketeers and the Sand Witch (C++ text based adventure)(2018)
Click to Download

"You and a member of the Saltsketeers are lost in the desert. Find your way back to the party before you subcumb to the Sand Witch."

This text based adventure is based in C++. Originally devloped to demonstrate knowledge of 'if' statements, it was expended to use indirect referencing of structures and strings. Due to limited time, only 4 saltsketeers are supported, with 8 desert creatures, 8 potions, And 8 miscelaneous items. However any number of characters and items could be implemented easily due to the game's flexible design.

P.S.: The name and theme of the game was inspired by a typo. FYI.




Mars Snowball Fight (C++ 2d sprite side-scroller)(2017)
Click to Download

"Mars is a cold place where wind and snow and aliens make a deadly combination. Stay sharp or you will never escape this frozen planet."

The Mars Snowball Fight series is an exercise in C++ programing with SDL and sprite sheets. Controll can be with the arrow keys or a game control pad. Art work was provided by Kathleen Tuck

Mars Snowball Fight: Shocks and Awe (C++ 2d sprite platformer)(2017)

"Shocks, the intrepid cyborg space exploring cat and his friends, have crash landed on Mars. Now they must use all their fancy "foot" skills to keep from the icey alien's grip"

This is part two of the Mars series. It is a ground up rewrite of the SDL handling code originally used for the Mars Snowball Fight. Although more flexible and expandable than its predicessor, the rewrite lead to a time overrun and the game was not fully functional. Once again, art work provided by Kathleen Tuck.
